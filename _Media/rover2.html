    Cal Mars Rover is nearing competition! We are recruiting new members to help finish up the prototype and learn from and engage with a highly knowledgeable group of graduate/ undergraduate students.

    We are having a general Meeting at 6:30PM this Weds. in Hesse. Contact aiaa.berkeley@gmail.com if you are interested. Thank you!

    March 2 ·

    The Cal Rover team was testing their 'Mars Rover' out on memorial glade yesterday morning.

    They will submit this design to the University Rover Challenge, which encourages student teams to build and design the next-generation of Mars rovers that will one day work alongside human explorers in the field.

    Winners will get a cash prize, a trip to present at the annual International Mars Society Convention and a year’s worth of bragging rights.
