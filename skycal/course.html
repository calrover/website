Course Info

CivEng 98/198 Private Pilot Ground School [2 units, P/NP]

Description: Perhaps it was the first time you saw an airplane fly across the sky and wondered what it's like to be a pilot. Or maybe you're curious about how planes fly and navigate the friendly skies. If you're interested in aviation for any reason, then you've caught the flying bug! And we have just the cure for that.

Learn to Fly: Private Pilot Ground School is a course designed to reveal the wonders of aviation to you right here in Berkeley and prepare you for the journey to become a Private Pilot.

Taking this course made me not only realize my aspiration to fly, but helped me to attain this goal. Fourty-Two flight hours later and I was a Private Pilot!
              ~Jeremy Axlerod

You'll learn the fundamentals and concepts to be a safe and competent Private Pilot. In the classroom, we will cover everything from airplane systems, aerodynamics, radio procedures, navigation, weather, and much more.

Prerequisites: None other than a good attitude and a will to learn.
Fun Fact: Did you know that having a good moral character is a FAA requirement (FAR §61.163c) to become an airline pilot?
