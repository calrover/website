Facilitators:

Aaron Wienkers
✈ Aaron.Wienkers [at] berkeley.edu (Webpage)

Aaron is a 4.5th-year (8th semester) Mechanical Engineering major, interested in the most requisite understanding in Aviation: Fluid Dynamics. This is his fifth semester teaching this course, shepherding it since it's nascency to all it's glory today. He received his Private Pilot's License in 2009 flying out of his home airport (KGRB) in Green Bay, WI. He has since been training for his Instrument Rating.
Out of the cockpit, Aaron enjoys hiking, playing tennis, and playing piano.


Jeremy Axlerod
✈ jaxelrod [at] berkeley.edu

Jeremy is a fourth-year Engineering Physics major. He was a student in this DeCal the first semester it was offered in the Fall of 2011, and the following summer he earned his Private Pilot's License flying out of KSIY in Montague, CA and KLMT in Klamath Falls, OR. He now flies out of both KCCR (Concord) and KOAK (Oakland) as frequently as possible, in pursuit of an Instrument Rating. He also has his tailwheel endorsement and enjoys aerobatics.
